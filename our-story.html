Building a more neighbourly world

We don’t just build houses. We create homes, exceptional spaces, and new communities where families love, grow and thrive.

We’re part of Peel L&P. That means that we’re passionate about our heritage in the North and proud to be a part of a company that has been transforming the region and enhancing people’s lives for over 40 years.

At Northstone, we innovate, adapt and regenerate spaces and places. We create sustainable, energy-efficient homes in community-focused spaces that shape the future of living.

Our ethos

We live for that feeling when you step through your door and into your own personal sanctuary. The contentment of a comfortable and relaxing space that works for you and your family.

We want our unique and intuitive design features to surprise you at first and then make you question how you ever did without them, while our spaces will delight, showcase the natural world, and encourage community.

Building communities

We’re passionate about creating spaces, homes and places for our Northern neighbours to live, thrive and grow.

Meet our team

The Northstone management team collectively has almost 150 years’ experience in the housebuilding and property industry.

Phil Wilson

Chief Executive

Phil has over 30 years' experience as a real estate professional and has worked in a variety of property sectors including large scale regeneration, low carbon energy and environmental, industrial as well as housebuilding.

He's passionate about creating new places and transforming environments.

Richard O'Brien

Design Director

Richard is our design guru and has spent most of his career in housebuilding, designing new homes and developments across the UK for many of the well-known housebuilders. He’s responsible for house type design, masterplans, layouts and landscaping.

Richard is an innovative creator of special places. He designs homes to maximise space and thoughtfully plans all designs elements to cater for the needs of modern living, promising functional and a simple aesthetic that accentuates any space.

Kevin Morris

Constuction Director

Kevin is our experienced, degree qualified Construction Director with over 35 years of experience in the industry.

He started off as an apprentice joiner and has worked on many sites, progressing through to managing them, as well as a stint working for the NHBC as a building inspector that provides warranties to homeowners for new housing. Kevin is a big team player and is passionate about encouraging training, professional development and apprenticeships.

Jon England

Development Director

Jon is a Chartered Civil Engineer with over 25 years’ experience in the housebuilding/construction and renewable energy industry. He has managed major highway, rail and aviation infrastructure projects as well as large and small scale housing developments.

Jon is a low carbon energy expert and a highly skilled project manager with a keen eye for detail.

Craig Nutter

Commerical Director

Craig is an accomplished quantity surveyor and our Commercial Director. He has over 15 years’ experience in a variety of construction and housebuilding companies. Craig is our main link to our sub-contractors and supply chain and is keen on team development and creating new standards.

He is our cost manager and makes sure things are done on time and to the right specification.
